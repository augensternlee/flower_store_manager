栖花漫雨

在这个四季分明的世界里，我总是能够感受到栖花漫雨带来的温柔和安宁。当我看着那些优美的雨滴滑落在花瓣上时，仿佛能够听见它们细碎的声音，这让我感到心灵的宁静和平静。

In this world of four distinct seasons, I always feel the tenderness and tranquility brought by the gentle rain and falling flowers. As I watch those beautiful raindrops slide down the petals, I can almost hear their gentle sound, which fills me with a sense of spiritual calm and serenity.
